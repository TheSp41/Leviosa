Burger

Description

A vegetarian burger, or veggie burger, is a meat-free patty served on a bun with traditional burger toppings. It can be a delicious and healthier alternative to a meat-based hamburger, with patties made from a variety of ingredients, including beans, grains, legumes, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Boiled and Mashed Potato
  • Chopped vegetables
  • Spices
  • Oil
  • Binding agents like breadcrumbs or poha
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Make the patties
  2. Cook the patties
  3. Prepare the sauce
  4. Assemble the burger
