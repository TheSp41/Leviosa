Dosa

Description

Dosa is a thin, crispy South Indian savory crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils (primarily black gram). Cooked on a hot griddle, it is a versatile dish, serving as a breakfast staple, a street food, or a light meal, and is traditionally enjoyed with chutneys and sambar.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Black lentil

Steps

  1. Prepare Dosa Batter
  2. Process rice and urad dal mixture in commercial grinder
  3. Salt to taste
  4. Cover mixture and let sit overnight
  5. Prepare Masala Dosa Filling
  6. Add mustard seeds and chana dal.
